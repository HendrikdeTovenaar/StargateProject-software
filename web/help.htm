Having trouble?

There are a few resources available to help you finish your 'Gate:

  1. Visit the FAQ on Kristian's Website
  2. Join our Discord server! There's a lively community of people just like you working to build their gates, and can answer questions. Email Kristian for an invite.
  3. Software problem or feature request? Visit the GitHub page and log an Issue.
